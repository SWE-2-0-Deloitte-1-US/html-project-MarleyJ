About Me

Born and raised in Georgia (Savannah & Atlanta). I've always had a passion for technlogy.
I have been playing video game since I was little, and I always wanted to make one of
my own. From there I self-studied Java and Python programming. After a bit, I came to
out that it would be more beneficial to know how a computer works before I can tell it
what to do. I began taking my passion for I.T more seriously. I purchased CompTIA A+
books and training guides to prep myself for the CompTIA A+ exam. At the same time,
I gained hands on experience by helping friends and family with technical issues. I
began to become more tech savvy as I learned company operations, processes, and advanced
my technical computer skills even further. As my experience grew, my opportunities did
as well. I went from being help-desk support to being an I.T Administrator - resolving
tickets and issues globally for companies. After completing the CompTIA A+ exam, I began
studying for my CompTIA Network+ certification. Currently I am on the brink of passing my
CompTIA Network+ exam and then I will be onto CompTIA Security+ or pick up my Java or Python
programming studies. I love everything about technology. I have a passion for creating and
helping people. Being able to bring my skills to the table and still be creative whilst doing
so. Whether I am rebuilding or repairing computers, remote support, or networking technicalit-
ies. I enjoy doing every bit of the job. Learning new technologies, company processes, and
meeting great people who have the same passion!
